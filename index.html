Introduction to me

I am learning how to code. I'm learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but I also have a lot of experience and knowledge of Microsoft Office (particularly Excel).

I live in Chester, UK.

My interests include coding, photography, birdwatching, writing verse, and walking my dog.

Since 2012, I've been volunteering with Theatre in the Quarter, which puts on musical plays in Chester. TiQ includes the children's drama-groups Jigsaw Music Theatre, Quartz Youth Theatre, and Rewind Youth Theatre. My work for them includes the design of posters and programmes, the writing of lyrics, and generally assisting with the running of the children's groups.
