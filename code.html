My coding story.

I have always been interested in getting computers to do neat stuff.

I self-taught myself a lot of advanced MS Excel functionality, in the development of sophisticated personal projects, including a Latin rhyming dictionary.

My formal education in the field was minimal. That changed in January 2019, when I joined Code Nation, a start-up that will teach you employable IT skills regardless of your prior level of experience.

Since then, I have progressed rapidly in my understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I designed this site, for instance, but I’ve also used React.js, Node.js, and Express.js.

Check out some of my projects, linked below! I am particularly pleased with my chess game, and my contributions to the chat-room and train-times apps.
